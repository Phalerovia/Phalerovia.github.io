CiberNetics

Phalerovia
 2022-05-23  44 words   One minute 
Contents

CiberNetics is a competition designed to introduce beginners to Red VS Blue Team competitions.

In this competition team of two people will compete with each other with the goal of gathering all the flags in the other team’s server, while protecting their own flag.
